How many product cards visible at once?

This controls how the carousel feels — tight & editorial vs. spacious & browseable

A — 1 card + edge peek

Like the reference screenshot. One dominant card fills most of the width, with just a sliver of the next card hinting at more. Matches the hero's single-slide feel. Editorial and bold.

B — 2 cards + edge peek

Two full cards visible side by side, with a partial third. More browseable, easier to compare products. More like a classic e-commerce carousel.

C — Centered active card

Active card is centered with partial cards visible on both sides. Like a stage/spotlight effect — previous and next are both hinted. Feels symmetrical and cinematic.
