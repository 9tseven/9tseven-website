Image stack layout — which style?

3 photos on the left side of the Manifesto section. How should they overlap?

A — Fan / Rotated Deck

Photos slightly rotated in opposing directions, fanned out like playing cards. Casual, editorial feel.

B — Diagonal Offset Stack

Each photo shifted slightly down-right from the next. Clean, ordered, like a pile of prints on a table.

C — Scattered / Organic

Photos at irregular positions and slight rotations. More spontaneous, like polaroids spread on a surface.
